🛡️ 組織性安全措施

  • 存取權限控管：採最小權限原則，僅授權人員可存取必要資料
  • 定期安全稽核：進行安全檢測與漏洞掃描
  • 資料備份：自動化備份機制，確保資料可復原性
  • 員工訓練：對處理個人資料的人員進行保密與安全教育

重要提醒：雖然我們已盡力保護您的資料安全，但無法保證絕對安全。請妥善保管您的帳號密碼，切勿與他人共享。若發現帳號異常，請立即聯絡我們。

We take the security of your personal data very seriously and implement industry-standard and best-practice technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Technical Security Measures

  • System-Level Encryption:

    Sensitive data uses SecureStore (hardware-level protection), large data uses SHA-256 obfuscation

  • Secure Transmission:

    All data transfers use HTTPS/TLS 1.2+ encrypted protocols

  • Password Protection:

    User passwords are hashed with one-way encryption; we cannot view original passwords

Important Notice: While we strive to protect your data, absolute security cannot be guaranteed. Please keep your account credentials safe and never share them with others. If you notice any account anomalies, contact us immediately.
